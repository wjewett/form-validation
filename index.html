Form Validation

Using Regular Expressions and Custom Validity Messages.

First and Last Name
Requires at least one space in between 2 groups of characters, 2 characters in length. More than two names are allowed.

E-mail Address
Requires one or more characters before at symbol, one or more characters after it, a period, and at least two more characters.
Strong Password
Requires a one lowercase and uppercase letter, number, and character. Minimum length of eight.
Username
Allows any letters or numbers. Special characters and spaces not allowed. Length of 6-20 characters.
Phone Number
10 numbers. Accepted with or without dashes.
Zip Code
5 digit zip code, with or without the +4 code.
